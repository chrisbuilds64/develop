Abstract minimalist logo for "PressRoom" — a content production command center app.

Design concept: A stylized printing press cylinder merged with a Kanban board grid. The cylinder transforms into 6 vertical columns flowing left to right, suggesting content moving through pipeline stages.

Color palette: Deep indigo (#4338CA) as primary with subtle gradients to electric blue (#448AFF). Accent glow in warm amber. Background: transparent/dark.

Style: Clean geometric, sharp edges softened by subtle rounded corners. No text, no literal objects — pure abstract form. Think: if Dieter Rams designed a newsroom icon.

Suitable as an app icon at 1024x1024. Single focal shape, works at 16px favicon size. No gradients that break at small sizes. High contrast silhouette.

Square format, 1:1 ratio.
